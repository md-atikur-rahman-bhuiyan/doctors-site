Adam C. Miller, DDS, MD

CEO, Medical Director

Dr. Miller is a dentist, surgeon, and integrative medicine expert who has trekked the globe looking for the heart of the matter. He founded ARISE MD to help bridge the gap between dental and medical practices, evolve treatment and medicine to allow patients to become their own best doctor with efficiency, physician-patient education, and their self efficacy. In his free time he enjoys exploring the outdoors, mountain biking, skiing, and appreciating art and music of all forms with his wife, Brittney. His faith is just one of the aspects that make him a unique doctor and man. He has a deep passion for helping others that goes beyond the four walls of the ARISE MD practice and celebrates meeting and talking to people from all walks of life.

Hannah Check

Practice Manager

Hannah is the practice manager of ARISE MD and joined the team 2 years ago after graduating from the University of Minnesota with a B.S. degree in Kinesiology. She manages not just the physical operations of the office but acts as a physician’s assistant to Dr. Miller as well as a patient liaison. She is currently furthering her education with a health coach certification course to become a Functional Diagnostic Nutrition Practitioner (FDN-P).

Working for Dr. Miller at Arise MD steered my interest towards helping others reach their peak health. I feel my very best when I am being active, traveling and learning about different cultures, and spending quality time with loved ones. I am proud to be a part of Arise MD and assisting Dr. Miller with all of the great work he does.
